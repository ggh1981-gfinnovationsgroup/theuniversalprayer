Ian, you are joy and hope

Jesus me ayudo y tambien te cuida a tiJesus helped me and He also takes care of you2026-07-11

Hijo, hoy un cable se cayo al fondo del pozo y yo no podia alcanzarlo. Le pedi ayuda a Dios y use un palo con un clavo. El clavo atrapo justo el cable correcto y lo pude sacar. Yo senti que Jesus y tu Angel de la Guarda nos ayudaron. Quiero que aprendas esto: cuando algo te preocupe, ora conmigo y dile a Jesus: te necesito.

Son, today a cable fell deep into the tank and I could not reach it. I asked God for help and used a stick with a nail. The nail caught the exact right cable and I could pull it out. I felt that Jesus and your Guardian Angel helped us. I want you to learn this: when something worries you, pray with me and tell Jesus: I need You.
