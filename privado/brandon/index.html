Fortaleza y proposito Strength and purpose 2026-07-11

Hijo, eres fuerte, valioso y llamado a una vida con proposito. Yo te amo con todo mi corazon, pero Dios y Maria te aman aun mas. Oro para que seas un hombre de palabra, responsable y fiel a Jesus.

Son, you are strong, valuable, and called to a life of purpose. I love you with all my heart, but God and Mary love you even more. I pray that you become a man of your word, responsible and faithful to Jesus.

Levantate cuando tropieces Rise when you stumble 2026-07-10

Cuando tengas miedo recuerda que Dios camina contigo. En esta casa siempre tendras amor, direccion y una familia que ora por ti.

When you are afraid, remember that God walks with you. In this home you will always have love, guidance, and a family that prays for you.
