Barbara, te amo con todo mi corazon

Barbara, I love you with all my heart

Hija, eres un regalo precioso de Dios para nuestra familia. Yo te amo con todo mi corazon, pero Dios y Maria te aman aun mas y cuidan cada paso de tu vida. Oro por ti todos los dias para que el Senor te regale sabiduria, pureza de corazon y fortaleza interior, para que nunca te falte la esperanza cuando lleguen pruebas. Que aprendas a reconocer tu valor en Cristo, a elegir amistades que te acerquen al bien y a construir tus suenos con disciplina, alegria y fe. Siempre estare contigo: para escucharte, abrazarte, corregirte con amor y recordarte que naciste para cosas grandes en la gracia de Dios.

Daughter, you are a precious gift from God to our family. I love you with all my heart, but God and Mary love you even more and watch over every step of your life. I pray for you every day, asking the Lord to give you wisdom, purity of heart, and inner strength, so hope will not fail you when trials come. May you learn to see your true worth in Christ, choose friendships that lead you toward what is good, and build your dreams with discipline, joy, and faith. I will always be with you: to listen, to embrace you, to correct you with love, and to remind you that you were born for great things in God's grace.
