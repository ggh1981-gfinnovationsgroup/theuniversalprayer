Dios responde incluso en el caos God answers even in chaos 2026-07-11

Hija, hoy tuve una dificultad grande en casa: el cable de la bomba del pozo cayo muy profundo y no alcanzaba a recuperarlo. Intente varias cosas y nada funcionaba. Cuando ya parecia perdido, ore y segui intentando con un palo que tenia un clavo, y ese clavo saco justo el cable correcto entre muchos. Para mi fue una gracia concreta de Dios. En la manana habia orado la coronilla del Sagrado Corazon y un rosario, y despues me confese. Tambien aparecio en el dia una frase de San Francisco de Sales: no podemos evitar que los pajaros vuelen sobre la cabeza, pero si que hagan nido. Quiero que recuerdes esto: las pruebas llegan, las tentaciones tambien, pero con oracion, sacramentos y decisiones firmes, Dios da luz y salida.

Daughter, today I faced a serious problem at home: the pump cable in the septic tank fell very deep and I could not reach it. I tried several things and nothing worked. When it seemed lost, I prayed and kept trying with a stick that had a nail, and that nail pulled out the exact cable among many. For me, this was a concrete grace from God. In the morning I had prayed the Sacred Heart chaplet and a rosary, and later I went to confession. During the day I also heard a quote from Saint Francis de Sales: we cannot stop birds from flying over our heads, but we can stop them from nesting there. Remember this: trials come, temptations too, but with prayer, sacraments, and firm choices, God gives light and a way forward.

Recuerda tu valor en Cristo Remember your worth in Christ 2026-07-10

No olvides que naciste para cosas grandes en la gracia de Dios. Siempre tendras mi amor, direccion y una familia que ora por ti.

Never forget that you were born for great things in God's grace. You will always have my love, guidance, and a family that prays for you.
