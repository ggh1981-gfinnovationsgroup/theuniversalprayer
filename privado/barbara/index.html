Barbara, te amo con todo mi corazon

Barbara, I love you with all my heart

Palabras de papa para hoy Dad's words for today 2026-07-11

Hija, eres un regalo precioso de Dios para nuestra familia. Yo te amo con todo mi corazon, pero Dios y Maria te aman aun mas y cuidan cada paso de tu vida. Oro por ti todos los dias para que el Senor te regale sabiduria, pureza de corazon y fortaleza interior.

Daughter, you are a precious gift from God to our family. I love you with all my heart, but God and Mary love you even more and watch every step of your life. I pray for you every day for wisdom, purity of heart, and inner strength.

Recuerda tu valor en Cristo Remember your worth in Christ 2026-07-10

No olvides que naciste para cosas grandes en la gracia de Dios. Siempre tendras mi amor, direccion y una familia que ora por ti.

Never forget that you were born for great things in God's grace. You will always have my love, guidance, and a family that prays for you.
