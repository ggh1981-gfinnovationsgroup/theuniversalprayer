Clarissa, eres alegria del cielo

Clarissa, you are joy from heaven

Hija, tu sonrisa es un regalo para nuestra familia. Yo te amo con todo mi corazon, pero Dios y Maria te aman aun mas. Oro para que seas siempre dulce, valiente y fiel a la verdad de Jesus.

Daughter, your smile is a gift to our family. I love you with all my heart, but God and Mary love you even more. I pray that you will always be gentle, brave, and faithful to the truth of Jesus.
