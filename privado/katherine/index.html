Katherine, eres luz y ternura

Katherine, you are light and tenderness

Hija, Dios te hizo con belleza y bondad. Yo te amo con todo mi corazon, pero Dios y Maria te aman aun mas. Oro para que crezcas con alegria, pureza y un corazon que siempre busque a Jesus.

Daughter, God made you with beauty and goodness. I love you with all my heart, but God and Mary love you even more. I pray that you grow with joy, purity, and a heart that always seeks Jesus.
