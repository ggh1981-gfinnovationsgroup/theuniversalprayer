Katherine, eres luz y ternura

Katherine, you are light and tenderness

Carta de amor: Jesus cuida tu corazonLove letter: Jesus takes care of your heart2026-07-11

Hija hermosa, hoy quiero ensenarte algo muy importante: cuando algo da miedo, primero se ora y luego se actua con paz. Dios siempre escucha a sus hijos y nunca llega tarde. Tambien aprendi que debemos cuidar lo que pensamos, porque no todo pensamiento viene de Dios. San Francisco de Sales ensena que podemos decirle no a los pensamientos malos y decirle si a la paz de Jesus. Tu eres muy amada. Ora, sonrie y confia: Jesus camina contigo todos los dias.

Beautiful daughter, today I want to teach you something very important: when something is scary, we pray first and then act in peace. God always listens to His children and is never late. I also learned that we must guard what we think, because not every thought comes from God. Saint Francis de Sales teaches that we can say no to bad thoughts and yes to the peace of Jesus. You are deeply loved. Pray, smile, and trust: Jesus walks with you every day.
