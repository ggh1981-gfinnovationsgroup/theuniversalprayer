Katherine, you are light and tenderness

Dios me ayudo y quiero que lo recuerdesGod helped me and I want you to remember it2026-07-11

Hija, hoy el cable de la bomba del pozo se cayo muy abajo y ya no lo alcanzaba. Le pedi ayuda a Dios y segui intentando con un palo que tenia un clavo. Ese clavo saco justo el cable correcto. Para mi fue un regalo del cielo. En la manana ore y despues me confese, y senti que Jesus ya me estaba cuidando antes del problema. Quiero que guardes esto en tu corazon: cuando algo te asuste, ora primero y confia, porque Dios nunca te deja sola.

Daughter, today the pump cable fell very deep into the septic tank and I could not reach it. I asked God for help and kept trying with a stick that had a nail. That nail pulled out the exact right cable. For me, it was a gift from heaven. In the morning I prayed and later I went to confession, and I felt Jesus was already caring for me before the problem. Keep this in your heart: when something scares you, pray first and trust, because God never leaves you alone.
